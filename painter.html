PAINTER AND WORK




This is an American illustrator. She is not the world famous painter. She is not Van Gogh, nor is Monet more than Da Vinci. She is just an ordinary woman. She works in a government agency, but she has always loved painting, and she doesn't love her current job. She starts traveling around the world, picks up her brushes, and goes wherever she goes. She travels with a paintbrush while others travel with her camera. What is striking is that she USES only small iron boxes, which are novel, convenient and effective. When I first saw these works, I liked them. Art comes from living, and pursuing what you love should be the best thing.
